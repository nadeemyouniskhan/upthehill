Game Developement Document

Game Title: Up The Hill

Executive Summary

Game Concept

The object of Up The Hill Racing is to drive as far through progressively difficult racing stages as possible while collecting coins, taking advantage of the nonrealistic physics and using only two simple controls: the Gas and Brake pedals. While in midair, pressing these pedals will instead cause the vehicle to rotate, allowing the player to control the angle with which they land. Fuel is replenished by picking up gas canisters or batteries along the way. The player can perform stunts such as driving the vehicle into the air for a prolonged time or flipping it over to earn more coins, which after the race may be spent on upgrades or to unlock new stages and vehicles (including monster trucks, dirt bikes, tanks).Conditions ending the game are depleting the vehicle's fuel or hitting the driving avatar's head.

Genre

Racing Video Games

Target Audiance

Our game Targeting both male & females, but mostly males ranging from the ages 10 – 35. Car Journalists, Car Enthusiasts and anyone who likes racing games in general.

Project Scope

To entertain and help people learn new strategies. The game will be an addictive and interactive game. it will have a simplistic two-button control and the freemium model(The term “freemium” is used to describe games that are free to play but require money to unlock certain features). The game leads to exciting adventures journey. The player controls a driver across hilly terrains, collecting coins along the way and spending them on vehicular upgrades. The Conditions of ending the game are depleting the vehicle's fuel or hitting the driving avatar's head.

Game Play

Objectives

  1. Easy game play, anyone can play it and don’t need to spend time learning the game
  2. No crashes, excellent user reviews
  3. Realistic and fluid physics engine
  4. Constant advancement through the use of coins.

Fuel

Fuel management plays a key part in the gameplay. As you drive, your fuel gauge will slowly deplete (keep in mind that the vehicles all have different sized fuel tanks). When you run out of fuel, you will roll to a stop and it's game over. You can extend your run by collecting the red fuel canisters (Fuel canister.png) to refill your fuel along the way. If there is a fuel canister nearby, you will see a countdown appear on the right-hand side of the screen showing how close you are to the fuel canister.
